EINAR VIÐAR GUÐMUNDSSON THORODDSEN

Einar is an Icelandic born graphic designer, visual artist, and illustrator based in The Hague. His playful style strikes an equilibrium between humour and seriousness, and his primary interests lie in print-making, illustration, and creating psychedelic explosions of colour. Einar draws inspiration from his surroundings—always searching for and creating textures, observing people's mannerisms, and embracing the imperfections of weathered or destroyed objects—for his printed works. Einar believes in the joy of continuous creation, even in failure, as that's where things get interesting.

Projects, drawings, books, zines, posters, prints...


A collection of projects and works from 2021 to 2023, either from school, applications, freelance or of my own interests or pursuits. Collaboration is a key aspect in some of these projects, and I hope to keep collaborating with as many different interesting individuals as I possibly can.
